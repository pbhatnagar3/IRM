2. How do I login to ITS? Do I need to be on the ECE network?

  • ITS is available at: its.vip.gatech.edu — login with GT id ( same as for T-square )
  • You do not need to be on the ECE network, it is accessible from anywhere.

3. What is the purpose of ITS?

ITS is a way to get extra practice on problems aside from homework. It tends to be relatively low-pressure and many students reported in surveys from past semesters that it was helpful.

  • Supplement homework questions with quick conceptual questions
  • Review & Test your conceptual knowledge of course material
  • Electronically grade and distribute problems

4. How are Assignments graded on ITS?

  • Each question is worth 100 pts, you need 2400 pts to obtain full score on each Assignment
  • Each Assignment consists of 40+ questions
  • ITS Assignment = Homework Assignment
  • ITS Assignment grade = 30*( min( Assignment_Points , 2400 ) / 2400 )
  • In ITS under the "My Scores" link you can view your grade for each Assignment

5. What is the opening/closing schedule for ITS Assignments?

  • Each assignment will be open for 2+ weeks, with ample time for problem solving activity
  • ITS scheduled to ( approximately ) open:

    Assignments 1-3 before Quiz 1
    Assignments 4-5 before Quiz 2
    Assignments 6-7 before Quiz 3
    Assignments 8 before the Final
